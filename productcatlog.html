Machine Learning

Course Overview
This course serves as an entry point into the fascinating field of machine learning, providing a comprehensive introduction to its fundamental concepts, techniques, and applications. Through a combination of lectures, hands-on exercises, and practical projects, students will gain the knowledge and skills necessary to understand, implement, and evaluate various machine learning algorithms.
